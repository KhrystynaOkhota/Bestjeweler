Return Policy

At Miro Jewelers we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you need to return or exchange your jewelry with in 7 days of purchase (in new condition) to the purchaser on file in the same form as original payment. Some exclusions may apply. Custom jewelry, designer jewelry, special orders, jewelry that has been modified, sized or engraved cannot be returned.

Prior to returning any merchandise, please contact our Customer Service Department by calling us at 303.732.8991, to obtain a Return Authorization Number (RA#)

All returns must be accompanied by the proof of purchase, diamond certificate, gem report, and all other original shipping documentation and packaging.

Returned merchandise will be inspected by Miro Jewelers prior to issuance of refunds. You will receive an email notification that the return process has begun. Any returned merchandise must be in its original condition, undamaged and unused. The security tag must be in place and intact. We are not able to accept any jewelry item for return that has any indication that it has been used, damaged, or that does not have the original tags attached (has not been removed at any time).

Returned merchandise must be shipped prepaid (via FedEx, UPS or U.S. Mail - Registered Delivery), and insured for the full purchase price to:

Miro Jewelers

Ref: Online Return (RA #)
6750 S. Emporia St.
Centennial, CO 80112
303.393.8881

Miro Jewelers will not be responsible for loss or damage of return shipments

**Please note, purchases made in our Centennial, Colorado store are subject to our standard 7 day return policy**
