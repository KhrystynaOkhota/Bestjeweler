About Us

Miro Jewelers has become an established family owned and operated jeweler in Colorado. We are a locally-owned and operated full-service jeweler in Centennial. With over 4,000 pieces in stores, Miro has an extensive selection of fine jewelry, and bridal jewelry, no matter what style you love. In addition, Miro specializes in custom design. If you can dream it, we can design it. Our staff is trained to listen and take the time to understand every customer’s needs. They are skilled to transform your vision to reality. Being jewelry manufacturers by trade and having a jeweler in house, we pride ourselves on carefully producing outstanding, quality pieces.

LOVING WHAT WE DO

Miro Jewelers provides free shipping on all your purchases! Miro Jewelers ships all items by FedEx and is insured for the full retail value. For your security, all packages will require an authorized adult signature. Please plan accordingly to have someone available to sign for the package. We are unable to ship packages to P.O. Box addresses.

Standard shipping is 2 days (Monday – Friday), but are able to provide overnight delivery based on specific circumstances.

If you have any questions or specific requests for shipping, please contact us at 303-732-8991 or at info@mirojewelers.com
