Shipping Information

Miro Jewelers provides free shipping on all your purchases! Miro Jewelers ships all items by FedEx and is insured for the full retail value. For your security, all packages will require an authorized adult signature. Please plan accordingly to have someone available to sign for the package. We are unable to ship packages to P.O. Box addresses.

Standard shipping is 2 days (Monday – Friday), but are able to provide overnight delivery based on specific circumstances.

If you have any questions or specific requests for shipping, please contact us at 303-732-8991 or at info@mirojewelers.com
