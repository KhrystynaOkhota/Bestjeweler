DESIGN YOUR OWN CUSTOM JEWELRY

With more than 56 years in jewelry manufacturing, custom design isn’t just a service, it’s a passion. At Miro Jewelers, every customer is unique and that one-of-a-kind piece expresses that uniqueness in ways that can’t be matched. When designing your custom piece, we encourage you to bring or upload pictures from magazines, your own sketches, and anything else that may inspire your imagination. Your input and ideas enable us to create a piece tailored to enhancing your personal characteristics.

Depending on the design, we will create the model by either hand-carving it from a block of wax, by our model maker, or by creating a model using computerized methods of jewelry design called Computer Aided Design and Computer Aided Manufacturing (CAD/CAM). Using this state-of-the-art software we can create, refine, and perfect designs until they meet the standards of our client’s vision. None of our work is outsourced and we seek your input throughout the development process to ensure an end product that can be worn with pride.

INITIAL DESIGN CONSULTATION
We will discuss and create the vision of your custom ring. At this point we would like you to send us any images, links, or pictures you have to help us from stepping stones to begin designing. We will discuss budget, design, ring size and center stone preferences.
CENTER STONE SELECTION
Once the ring and the center stone is finalized, Miro Jewelers requires a 50% deposit. At this step the order will move into production. Miro Jewelers accepts all major credit cards, checks, bank wire transfers and PayPal. We also provide no interest financing and layaway plans.
RENDITION
Once the center stone has been picked, we begin designing. We will send you multiple views and angles of the ring design until the ring is perfect. We can modify and make changes till the ring is perfect.
DEPOSIT AND PAYMENT
Using all the guidelines you have given us, we will begin the perfect filtration of our large inventory of diamonds and colored stones and set aside the stones that we believe will bring your design to life. Upon selection we will schedule a showing via email or in person where we will review every option with you.
PRODUCTION
We ask to give us 2-4 weeks to create each custom made ring, however we cab accommodate most time frames as long as it is discussed prior to production. If you need the ring sooner please let us know, there is always a way we can make it happen because all manufacturing is done in house.
COMPLETION & DELIVERY
Once your ring is complete, you are welcome to approve the ring in person or via email. We can send you pictures and videos. Once approved and paid in full, you can pick up the piece from our Centennial, Colorado showroom or have it shipped via fully insured next day air shipping.
